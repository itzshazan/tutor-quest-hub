

# Tutor Quest Landing Page

A comprehensive, modern landing page for the Tutor Quest tutoring platform with 10 distinct sections.

## Sections to Build

1. **Navigation Bar** — Sticky header with logo, nav links (Home, Find Tutors, Become a Tutor, How It Works), Login/Sign Up buttons, and mobile hamburger menu.

2. **Hero Section** — Bold headline "Find Trusted Local Tutors Near You", subtext, inline search bar (Subject, Location, Grade Level dropdowns + Find Tutors button), and two CTA buttons.

3. **Tutor Search Preview** — Filter bar (Subject, Location, Budget, Rating) with a grid of sample tutor cards showing name, subject, distance, rating, and "View Profile" button.

4. **How It Works** — 4-step visual flow: Search → Compare → Contact → Schedule, with icons and brief descriptions.

5. **Platform Features** — 4-card grid: Location-Based Search, Verified Profiles, Direct Communication, Flexible Scheduling — each with an icon and description.

6. **Featured Tutors** — Carousel/grid of top-rated tutor cards with photo, name, subject, experience, rating, distance, hourly rate, and action buttons.

7. **Benefits Section** — Two-column layout: "For Students" (3 benefits) and "For Tutors" (3 benefits) with icons.

8. **Tutor Registration CTA** — Full-width banner with headline "Are you a Tutor? Start Teaching Students in Your Area" and a prominent "Become a Tutor" button.

9. **Testimonials** — Cards with quotes from a student, parent, and tutor with avatars and names.

10. **Footer** — Multi-column footer with Company, Resources, Legal links, and social media icons.

## Design & UX
- Clean, modern design with a warm education-themed color palette (blues/greens)
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll between sections
- All data is static/mock (no backend)
- Navigation links scroll to corresponding sections on the page
